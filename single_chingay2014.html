Chingay 2014

Photography

Photo Shooting for Annual Float Parade, Singapore

Project Description

This series of photos were taken during Singapore's annual float, Chingay (“妆艺大游行”), in 2014. Since there was one float representative from NUS participating in Chingay 2014, as a member of residence hall level photography club, I was assigned the duty to take photos for this event.

That was my first experience of photographing in such fast-paced environment, dealing with dramatic lighting. For a beginner photographer, it was challenging yet unexpectedly exciting. Below are the photos of the 2-night parade.
