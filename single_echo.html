ECHO

Photography & Photobook Project

Individual Project of VA329/547 Photography & Expression, AY2014/2015, SEM II

Sabanci University

Final Product Preview

Concept Elaboration

As a product of my individual project under the course "Photography & Expression", "Echo | 回响" is an artist book project by using photography and text as the main expression tools. The core concept of this photo book is developed from my thinking about the duality of death and life after my grandfather passed away.

Considering “7” is a meaningful number in Chinese numerology and funeral customs, this photo book will probably consists of 28 (a full cycle of moon phases) pictures. Each of these pictures will focus on one specific signifier object which signifies a certain aspect related to “duality”.
